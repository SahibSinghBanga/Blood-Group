About
This web application is all about finding people of a particular blood group existing in a particular state.

This application comes in use when a person needs a blood and currently that blood group is not available. People having blood with same bloodgroupmay available nearby and they may be willing to donate. But how that person comes to know about those people? Is there any way? Exactly this is the way how our application works.

After registration he/she will become the member of our blood donation team. It means now he can search people of a particular bloodgroup and can be searched by other people if they need blood of same blood group.

The main motive is to connect people, so that they can serve each other in an emergency.
